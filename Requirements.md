## Problem Statement

Millions of citizens struggle to access government schemes, scholarships, healthcare programs, and public services due to:

- Complex government portals
- English-heavy documentation
- Low digital literacy
- Poor internet connectivity
- Lack of awareness

Access to information exists, but accessibility is limited.

---

## Who It Affects

- Rural populations
- Elderly citizens
- Non-English speakers
- First-generation learners
- Low-income households
- Small farmers and informal workers

---

## Why It Matters

- Eligible citizens miss benefits
- Government resources remain underutilized
- Digital divide increases inequality
- Public systems fail to reach intended beneficiaries

Improving access increases inclusion, transparency, and civic participation.

---

## Proposed Solution

Saarthi AI is a multilingual, voice-first civic assistant that helps users:

- Discover relevant government schemes
- Check eligibility
- Understand application processes
- Generate required document checklists
- Receive reminders and alerts

The system simplifies complex public information into conversational guidance.

---

## Core Features

- Multilingual regional language support
- Voice-based interaction
- AI-powered scheme recommendation
- Automated eligibility checking
- Step-by-step application guidance
- Personalized document checklist
- Deadline alerts and updates
- Low-bandwidth optimization
- Secure, consent-based data handling
